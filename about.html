Who We Are?

DEL ROSE SARI-SARI STRORE is owned by Adelaida Esplana a 59-year-old, born on January 15, 1964, a businesswoman from Brgy. Punong Santa Cruz, Marinduque. She was an undergraduate student of Luzonian University now called Enverga University in Lucena City, Quezon Province. She took a BS in Zoology for only one sem and then she shifted to another course, and she took a BS in Commerce, after one year she shifted to another course, and then she took a BS in Civil Engineering for two years. After that, she went to Bulacan and worked there as a tailor for a company. In the year 2000, she went back to Lucena City and started a business, and after one year she went back home to Marinduque to start another business, and in the year 2001, DEL ROSE SARI-SARI STORE was born. DEL ROSE SARI-SARI STORE is located at Brgy. Pag-asa Santa Cruz, Marinduque. The store has a lot to offer, from dishes, snacks, beverages,and other items that you can buy.
